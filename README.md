Hardware Hack
THEME — THE WORLD IS YOUR CONTROLLER

Physical interfaces have always relied on buttons and screens. Your challenge is different. Use the MPU-6050 to read motion, orientation, and gesture from the real world and translate that data into physical action through the SG90 servo, LEDs, and the serial terminal output. Build firmware that makes hardware feel alive — gesture-driven, motion-reactive, and running entirely on the metal.
